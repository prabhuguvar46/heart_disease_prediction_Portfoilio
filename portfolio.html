Comprehensive Health Prediction Features

Risk Assessment

he system evaluates various risk factors such as age, gender, cholesterol levels, blood pressure, ECG images. It uses a combination of these inputs to assess the overall risk of heart disease, providing a more accurate prediction by considering the interplay between multiple factors.

Machine Learning-Powered Analysis

Utilizing advanced machine learning algorithms, the system can analyze large datasets to identify patterns and correlations that may not be apparent through traditional statistical methods. This allows for personalized risk prediction and the continuous improvement of the model’s accuracy as more data is fed into the system.

User-Friendly Interface

The platform features an intuitive interface where users can easily input their data and receive immediate results. The system provides detailed insights, including the probability of heart disease, personalized recommendations for lifestyle changes, and alerts for high-risk users to seek medical advice promptly.
